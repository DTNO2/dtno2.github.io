Team Norms

27 Sep 2017 • team
  1. Respect one another.
    1. Respect others’ ideas and opinions, attacking others in any ways is banned.
    2. Respect others’ speaking, do not interrupt others.
    3. Respect others’ work, laughing at poor works is banned.
    4. Respect others’ private life.
  2. Work effectively and efficiently.
    1. No chatting on subjects that has no link to the work.
    2. Participate in the topic and brainstorming actively.
    3. Always be careful and focused on what you do.
  3. Think different
    1. Think critically.
    2. Doubt the assumptions.
    3. Everyone should express his own opinion.
  4. Time should be well planned.
    1. Control the time of every part in case time is not enough finally.
    2. Have a try bravely, act undoubtfully.
  5. Safety and Happiness is No.1 priority.

Signee (in alphabetic order): Jack R. Liu, Qiming Lu, Daisy Qiu, Jim Wang, Yehao Zhang.
